SLS_ROBUSTER v1.5.4
-------------------

Visual & UI Changes

The Schedule tab has been completely redesigned. Classes are now shown on a
vertical timeline from 8 AM to 5 PM, with each chip placed at its actual start
time and sized proportionally to its duration. This replaces the old slot-card
grouping which broke whenever a class spanned two periods.

Family Center and Chapel classes appear in side-by-side columns so you can see
both locations at a glance. When a time slot has only a Family Center class
(no overlapping Chapel class), its chip automatically spans both columns and
displays a "Combined" badge.

Faint horizontal grid lines appear every 15 minutes across the timeline to make
time alignment easy to read at a glance. Hour boundaries are slightly more
visible than the in-between markers.
