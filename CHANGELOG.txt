SLS_ROBUSTER v1.5.1
-------------------

Under the Hood

Update workflow test release. Validates the end-to-end in-app auto-update flow,
including download, extraction, and relaunch via the "Check for Updates" button.

Fixed: In-app updater now automatically forward-fills any new keys from the
bundled app_config.json into the user's installed config on every launch,
so settings added in newer versions reach existing installs without manual editing.
