SLS_ROBUSTER v1.6
-----------------

Under the Hood

Introduced SLS_Launcher — a small companion app that permanently solves the
macOS Gatekeeper block on auto-updates. After approving the launcher once in
System Settings > Privacy & Security, all future updates install and relaunch
automatically with no further prompts.

The main app bundle is now always named SLS_ROBUSTER.app on disk (no version
number in the filename). The version badge inside the app continues to show the
exact version. This stable name is what allows the launcher to find and open the
correct app after each update.
